About

I am a PhD Candidate in Political Science at the University of Toronto, studying state‑business relations under authoritarianism. I’m also the co‑founder of a Toronto‑based consultancy company that leverages social media data and quantum computing to predict political risks for businesses. Prior to my doctoral studies, I served as a social policy consultant. I also co‑founded two social impact initiatives for youth empowerment, one based in Montreal that advocates for youth inclusion in the design of smart cities, and the other based in Istanbul that mobilizes more than 30 stakeholder organizations in six countries across the MENA region to foster youth employment. I’m a non‑resident fellow at Massey College.
